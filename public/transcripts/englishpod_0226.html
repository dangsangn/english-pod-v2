Key Vocabulary

raise
increase in size, quantity, or worth
door to door
from one house to the next
driveway
short private road from a street to a house
sponsor
agree to pay for an event or activity
donation
the act of giving to a fund or cause
solicit
seek to obtain by persuasion, entreaty, or formal
fundraiser
a person or an action which collects money for particular purposes
