The Weekend - Ordering At An Italian Restaurant (C0297)

A
Good evening ladies. My name is Josh and I'll be your server tonight. May I take your order?
B
Do you have any recommendations?
A
Well, I personally like the chicken penne with cream mushroom sauce, but the prawn fettuccine is also very nice.
B
Hmm. I'd like to have the grilled chicken, but can I have spaghetti instead of penne?
A
Of course, mam. And for you?
C
I... ah..I'll have the horse tripe.

Key Vocabulary

server
a waiter or waitress
recommendation
a suggestion or proposal
prawn
a large shrimp
fettuccine
pasta in the shape of long narrow strips
spaghetti
pasta dish originally from Italy
penne
a type of Italian pasta
horse tripe
the stomach of a horse used as food

Supplementary Vocabulary

parmesan
a type of cheese, usually goes with spaghetti
pizza
an Italian dish, round in shape
calzone
a turnover made of pizza dough, usually containing cheese
ricotta
a soft Italian cheese that resembles cottage cheese.
